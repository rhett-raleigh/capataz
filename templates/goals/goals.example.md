# Goals

Personal goals tracked by the capataz goal skills (`goal-checkin`, `goal-review`,
`goal-notify`, `morning-briefing`). Fill in the fields under each goal; delete
example text. Add or remove goals freely — the skills read whatever is here.

**Fields per goal:**
- **Target** — what "on track" means, concretely and measurably.
- **Why** — the motivation. Used by review/notify skills to phrase nudges.
- **Timeline** — end date or "ongoing".
- **Check-in cadence** — how often you intend to log (e.g. daily, 3x/week, weekly).
  Notifications and briefing flags key off this.
- **Status** — `active` or `paused`. Paused goals are never nudged.

## Notification settings

Used by `goal-notify`. Adjust to taste.

- **Quiet hours:** 22:00–08:00 (no notifications in this window)
- **Streak milestones:** 7, 14, 30, 60, 100 (then every 100)
- **Max notifications:** 1 per goal per day; multiple triggers bundle into one
  notification
